The History of our hotel

Built in the 1800s as a hub for travellers and traders at the time, our hotel symbolises stability and our commitment to be a refuge of tranquility and relaxition for tourists in this modern age. With unique sights along the Indian ocean scenes of the East African sunset on our mid-deck sun bathing spot are to die for. So come indulge yourself in some swahili cuisine and experience swahili hospitality. Halstills, your home away from home.
